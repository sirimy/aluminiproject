About

Objectives

"The richest people in the world look for and build networks. Everyone else looks for work." – Robert Kiyosaki

The Alumni of NHGWS was formally established on 1st April, 2015 with the primary objective of providing a vibrant platform for interaction and networking among alumni of the N H Goel World School

Other Objectives:

  • To foster linkages among the alumni, present students and faculty of the Institute
  • To develop a sense of belonging among alumni towards their alma mater, and to foster the old and healthy traditions of the Institution
  • To reinforce relationships developed during school years through meetings and get-togethers among members of the Association
  • To help alumni achieve their professional goals by leveraging the network
  • To identify and showcase successes of the alumni
  • To help alumni in the hour of crisis
  • To involve alumni in student development and mentorship activities through seminars, guest lectures, workshops, etc.
